Title & Escrow Services

Welcome to the Title & Escrow Services Page! You may be wondering, what makes Atlas Title & Escrow unlike any other settlement company out there? Well, think of it like this – a real estate transaction can be one of the most stressful processes, but it doesn’t have to be if you have the right team beside you. You should be able to sit back and relax while your team effortlessly handles the entire transaction and now, you can. This is no longer a dream – this is what we offer. Atlas Title & Escrow provides full settlement escrow services from start to finish. This includes: Immediate preliminary figures • 24/7 Access to your transaction progress • Quick turnaround on title • Qualia – a digital, secure platform • Obtain Municipal requirements based on guidelines • Fast responses to email • Quality Control and Accuracy on files We e-record online to deliver a quicker and more efficient document recording for our clients. Our services are consumer-based by the way we protect the information provided. We utilize a secure platform, Qualia, that has the same security built in system that banks use. The login is user-friendly and provides 24/7 access to their file progress. We are headquartered in Oakbrook, Illinois and can close in multiple locations throughout the Chicagoland area. Attorneys

Our services allow attorneys to feel supported and pleased because they have an experienced settlement company that delivers excellence. Atlas Title & Escrow assists in clearing any clouds on title and ensures proper processes in obtaining documentation. We obtain releases internally as well as lien clearing services. Additionally, we handle requesting and attaining water balances, zoning, municipality requirements, and stamp pickup. Let us do the busy work for you. But wait – we don’t just stop there. We conduct remote closings with our notary agents so the consumer does not have to come to us; we have multiple offices throughout the Chicago area. If they prefer to hold settlement in our headquarters, we have a brand new office located in Oakbrook, IL. After the transaction is completed, we also guarantee to provide document recording services and delivery. Lender

Lenders direct the transaction and require an adroit, knowledgeable title company. Here at Atlas Title & Escrow, we work hand in hand with the Lender so all parties feel comfortable and at ease because they are being taken care of. We thoroughly follow ALTA’s Best Practices when handling a transaction; in fact, our digital platform is built based on these best practices. The consumer comes first and we can ensure them their information is protected using a SOC-2 security system, similar to what banks use. Qualia also allows anyone associated to the real estate contract to login and follow the status of their file. We know the most important thing during a transaction is communication. Atlas Title & Escrow team members respond quickly to emails and phone calls, can provide title work within a week of contract receipt, and can produce immediate preliminary figures upon request. Having an experienced title company to work with can mitigate any closing delays because we work fast and we close successfully. We build lasting, productive relationships with lenders so every transaction goes smoothly for the consumer.
